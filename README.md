# Business-Insights-360-Power-BI-Dashboard


📊 Business Insights 360 | Power BI Project


📌 Project Overview


Business Insights 360 is a multi-functional Power BI dashboard project developed for AtliQ Hardware, an imaginary consumer electronics company operating across multiple countries.As the business expanded,
AtliQ Hardware continued relying on Excel-based reporting, which became inefficient for handling growing volumes of business data. This made it difficult for leadership teams to consume reports,
track performance effectively, and generate timely insights for decision-making. To overcome these challenges, this project delivers a centralized and interactive Business Intelligence solution that enables stakeholders to monitor and analyze business performance across Finance, Sales, Marketing, Supply Chain, and Executive Management.


🎯 Problem Statement


AtliQ Hardware is a fast-growing consumer goods electronics company with operations in multiple countries.Despite its rapid growth, the company still relied heavily on Excel files for analytics and reporting. While Excel can be useful for basic reporting, it becomes increasingly difficult to manage and scale when business data grows across regions, customers, products, and departments.


This dependency on Excel created several business challenges:


1. Reports were difficult to consume and interpret
2. Insights were not generated quickly enough for decision-making
3. Cross-functional business performance lacked centralized visibility
4. Leadership teams could not effectively monitor key KPIs
5. The absence of strong analytics contributed to a major business loss in Latin America


Recognizing the need for a more effective reporting system, the senior executives at AtliQ Hardware decided to invest in a data analytics initiative and assigned a team to build a scalable business intelligence solution.This project represents that solution.


🧩 Project Objective


The goal of this project was to build a comprehensive Power BI dashboard that transforms raw business data into meaningful insights and helps decision-makers monitor performance across the organization.


The dashboard was designed to:


1. Replace static Excel-based reporting with an interactive BI solution
2. Create a single source of truth for business performance
3. Improve decision-making with clear and actionable insights
4. Track KPIs across multiple business functions
5. Enable leadership to identify risks, opportunities, and performance gaps
